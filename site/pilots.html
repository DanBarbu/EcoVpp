What every pilot ships with

Tenant isolation

Dedicated Kubernetes namespace, dedicated PostgreSQL schema, per-tenant DIDs. One Helm release per pilot.

Local override

Hardware override pin on every node. Residents can opt out at any time without taking the building offline.

Operator dashboard

Real-time view of ingest rates, RED II shares, dispatch incentives, and on-chain settlement status.

Audit-ready

MiCA-compliant settlement log out of the box. Every share has an immutable Merkle proof.
