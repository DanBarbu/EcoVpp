Features.

Everything analogreader does, in one page. From the snap-and-go OCR to the open API your accountant will love.

Capture

📷

Phone camera

One-tap PWA. Works on any phone with a browser, no app store install needed.

📥

Drag-and-drop upload

Drop a photo from your phone, tablet, or desktop. JPEG, PNG, HEIC.

📡

ESP32 / Raspberry Pi

Mount a cheap camera in front of the meter and push readings via the open API. Reference firmware on GitHub.

📥

Bulk CSV import

Migrating from a spreadsheet? Import years of historical readings in one upload.

Read

⚡

Cyclometer + LCD electricity

5–6 digit dials, multi-tariff LCDs (T1/T2/off-peak).

🔥

Gas (m³)

Standard 5-digit dial; ignores the small auxiliary pointers.

💧

Water (m³ + sub-dials)

Cold and hot, with optional manual tagging if both share a photo.

🎯

Confidence gates

Auto-accept ≥ 0.95, audit 0.80–0.95, review < 0.80. Configurable per-meter.

✋

Manual override

Don't trust the OCR? Type it. The original photo is preserved for audit.

🔁

Periodic capture

Got a fixed-camera setup? Schedule readings every 15 min, hourly, or daily.

Track

📈

Daily / monthly / yearly charts

Stacked by utility, compared against last year, with weather overlay for heating loads.

💶

Live bill projection

Set your tariff (kWh/€, m³/€) and see the running cost. Cap and trip alerts if you blow the budget.

🚨

Leak detection

Flat baseline overnight gas/water consumption → instant alert. Catches drips before the bill arrives.

🏠

Multi-property

Track your home, your rental, your vacation place, your parents' place — all under one account.

Share & report

📄

PDF + CSV exports

Bill-period reports formatted for the utility's preferred format (UK, EU, US).

🔗

Read-only share links

Send a signed URL to your landlord or building manager. Expires automatically.

🔌

Open API

JSON + Webhooks. Pull readings into Home Assistant, Grafana, Power BI, or a spreadsheet.

📨

Email reports

Monthly digest in your inbox. Set the day, set the recipients, done.

For pros

🏢

Building manager view

Roll up readings across an entire condominium. Per-apartment access tokens. RED II energy-sharing exports.

🤝

Utility submission

Integrations with select utilities to submit your reading on bill day. No more postcards or call-centre wait times.

🧰

White-label

Use analogreader's OCR under your own brand. SLA-backed plans available — get in touch.

Wired with the EcoVpp platform

analogreader is the consumer front-end to the open-source EcoVpp orchestration platform. The OCR pipeline, settlement layer, and energy-sharing logic are all the same code. If you run a Virtual Power Plant, you get a consumer-grade meter UI for free.
