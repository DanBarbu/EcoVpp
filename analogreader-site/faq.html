The product

Does this replace my utility's meter?

No — it sits on top of it. Your physical meter stays exactly where it is. We just turn its analog face into a digital reading you can track, share, and export.

How accurate is the OCR?

Across our 12,000-photo benchmark of European meters, accuracy is 99.2% on clear, well-lit photos. Anything where the model isn't confident lands in a verification queue.

What if the OCR is wrong?

You can correct any reading in two taps. The corrected value becomes part of the training data (with your permission). The original photo is preserved for audit.

Can I track multiple buildings?

Yes. Pro plan covers unlimited meters across unlimited properties. Building managers should use the Team plan for proper per-apartment access tokens.

Privacy & data

Where are my photos stored?

Encrypted at rest in Frankfurt, Germany, by default. Enterprise plans can choose another region or run fully on-prem.

Do you sell my data?

No. Ever. Not aggregated, not anonymised, not for "research partners". analogreader is paid for by Pro/Team/Enterprise subscriptions, not by your data.

Can I delete everything?

Settings → Account → Erase. One click, immediate purge of photos and readings, account closed within 24 hours. We keep an irreversible cryptographic anchor of the deletion event for audit but no personal data.

Is this GDPR-compliant?

Yes. Full DPA available on request. EU users never have data leave the EU.

Integrations

Can I push readings to Home Assistant / Grafana?

Yes — via the open API (Pro and up). Webhooks supported for push, REST/JSON for pull. API docs.

Does it work with smart meters too?

For displaying side-by-side, yes. For replacing the smart meter reading entirely, you don't need analogreader — your utility already does the digital read. We exist for the millions of analog meters still on European walls.

Can I run a fixed camera and capture automatically?

Absolutely. Reference firmware for ESP32-S3 + camera on GitHub. Push readings via the API on whatever schedule you like.
