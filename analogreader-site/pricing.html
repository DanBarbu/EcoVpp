Frequently asked

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are month-to-month with no minimum term. We don't auto-charge for the year unless you opt in to the discounted annual plan.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Existing readings stay accessible read-only. You just can't add new ones beyond the Free quota until you upgrade again. Nothing is ever deleted without your explicit request.

Do you accept SEPA / invoice billing?

Card and SEPA are standard. Annual invoice billing is available on Team and Enterprise plans.

Is there a discount for non-profits or housing co-ops?

Yes — 50% off Team for registered co-ops and non-profits. Email us with your registration number.

Where is data stored?

Frankfurt, Germany by default. EU users never have data leave the EU. Enterprise plans can choose a specific region or run on-prem.
