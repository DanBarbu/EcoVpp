How it works

From snapshot to digital log in under five seconds.

1

Snap a photo

Phone camera or webcam. We auto-detect the meter face and align it.

2

AI reads it

On-device or cloud OCR returns the value plus a confidence score. Low-confidence readings are queued for quick human review.

3

See your trends

Daily, weekly, monthly charts. Compare against last year, get bill projections, export CSV.

4

Share or report

One-click report to your utility, your landlord, or your accountant. Photos and certificates stored as evidence.
