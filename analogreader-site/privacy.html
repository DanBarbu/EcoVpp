Privacy.

Plain-English summary, then the formal policy. The TL;DR is: your photos are yours, we don't sell anything, we store in the EU, you can erase everything with one click.

What we collect

  • Account: email, hashed password. Optional name + organisation.
  • Meter photos: only what you upload. Stored encrypted, never shared.
  • Readings: the OCR result + confidence + timestamp + device tag.
  • Operational logs: standard web-server logs (IP, user-agent, URL) kept 30 days for abuse prevention.

What we don't do

  • Sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers, brokers, or "research partners".
  • Train public AI models on your photos without explicit opt-in.
  • Send personal data to non-EU servers (unless your account is explicitly outside the EU).

Your rights

Access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction, objection — all in Settings → Account, or by emailing privacy@analogreader.online. We aim to respond within 5 business days.

Data residency

Primary hosting in Frankfurt, Germany. Backups in Dublin, Ireland. Enterprise customers can pin to a specific region or run on-prem.

Cookies

One first-party session cookie when you're logged in. No third-party trackers, no analytics by default. Pro / Team accounts can opt in to anonymous product analytics.
